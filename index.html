ITALY

Highlights :  Iconic cities like Rome, Florence, and Venice; world-class art, architecture, and cuisine.

Best For :  History buffs, food lovers, and art enthusiasts.

  • Florence

    • Discover Renaissance art and architecture.

  • Rome

    • Explore ancient ruins like the Colosseum and the Vatican.

JAPAN

Highlights :  A blend of traditional culture and modern technology in cities like Tokyo and Kyoto; stunning natural landscapes.

Best For :  Cultural experiences, technology enthusiasts, and nature lovers.

  • Tokyo

    • Experience the vibrant city life, shopping, and unique cuisine.

  • Kyoto

    • Visit beautiful temples and traditional tea houses.

NEW ZEALAND

Highlights :  Breathtaking landscapes, adventure sports in Queenstown, and unique Maori culture.

Best For :  Outdoor enthusiasts and adventure seekers.

  • Queenstown

    • Adventure sports and stunning landscapes.

  • Rotorua

    • Geothermal activity and rich Maori culture.

THIALAND

Highlights :  Vibrant street markets in Bangkok, cultural experiences in Chiang Mai, and stunning beaches in Phuket.

Best For :  Budget travelers, adventure seekers, and food lovers.

  • Bangkok

    • Bustling city life and vibrant street food.

  • Phuket

    • Gorgeous beaches and water activities.

Highlights :  Beautiful islands like Santorini and Crete, rich history in Athens, and delicious Mediterranean cuisine.

  • Athens
